⚙️ How it works

Four simple steps from idea to finished print.

1

Send me your model

Share a file (STL, OBJ, 3MF) or a link to any model site. No model? Describe what you want and I'll find one.

2

Get a price estimate

I'll calculate material, print time, and complexity and send you a clear quote with no hidden fees.

3

Pay securely via Finn.no

All payments go through Fiks Ferdig on Finn.no. Buyer and seller protection for both of us. No cash, no risk.

4

Receive your print

I print your model and ship it to you. You'll be notified when it's on its way.
